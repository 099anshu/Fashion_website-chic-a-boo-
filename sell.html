Guidelines for Selling

Clothing Condition & Quality Standards

  • ✔ Only Sell Wearable Clothes – Items should be clean and undamaged.
  • ✔ No Stains, Holes, or Odors – Ensure fresh and quality clothing.
  • ✔ Authentic & Original Products – Avoid counterfeits.

Image Upload Guidelines

  • ✔ Good Lighting – Use bright or natural light.
  • ✔ Multiple Angles – Upload 3-5 images.
  • ✔ Avoid Filters – Show the true color.
  • ✔ Highlight Any Defects – Be transparent.
